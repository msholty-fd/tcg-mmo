How to Duel

Each turn: draw a card, then act. Kindle a card (once per turn) to raise your permanent Ember — cards are your resource AND your fuel; choosing what to burn is the game.
Play creatures and attack with them next turn (or now, with Ambush). Strike the enemy Hearth to 0 to win.
Reactions are set face-down (max 2) and spring automatically on the event they name — enemy attacks, creatures, or spells. Your opponent sees that you set something, not what.
Enchantments are cast face-up (max 4) and stay in play for the rest of the duel — an aura or a repeating effect that fires again and again on the event it names, unlike a Reaction it's never used up.

🗡 Ambush — attacks the turn it's played · 🛡 Guardian — must be attacked first · ✨ Ward — untargetable by spells · ⚡ Frenzy — attacks twice · 🩸 Lifesteal — attacks restore Hearth · 🎯 Piercing — kill overflow hits the Hearth

Cards remember. Duels earn a copy Renown: Seasoned → Veteran (+1/+1) → Storied (+2/+2 + keyword). Decks carry at most 8 Legend points (Veteran 1, Storied 2).
